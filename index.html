Instructions:

You will be given a number of cards to buy - your "goal."

Each envelop has a specific number of cards in it.

Click on an envelope to add its quantity of cards to your cart.

The number of cards in each envelope will remain hidden the entire game.

(hint: look at how your score changes when you click an envelope.)

To win, you must get the same number of cards in your cart as the goal.

Get more cards than the goal and you lose.

Good Luck!
